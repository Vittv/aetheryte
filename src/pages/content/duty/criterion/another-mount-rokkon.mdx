## Gorai

- **Seal 1**: TM N / HR S, dps flex if stacks do not work.
- **Thundercall**: TH center (tank in front), DPS to either side.
- **Rousing Reincarnation 1**: Stack south and have `2s` & `4s` step in to bait tethers. Preposition with tether in your next tower; tower soakers grab nearest tether.
- <BulletLink href={data.planners[2].url}>Exaflares Raidplan</BulletLink> - TH N / MR S, melees flex.
- <BulletLink href={data.planners[3].url}>Limit Cut Raidplan</BulletLink>
- **Rousing Reincarnation 2**: <BulletLink href={data.planners[4].url}>RR2 Toolbox</BulletLink>

## Moko

- <BulletLink href={data.planners[6].url}>Scarlet</BulletLink> - Role pairs, tether to the corner.
- **Shadow Twin 1**: Role pairs if possible, otherwise use eyes. Go to safe spot by your tether add or rotate CW if N/S safe.
- **Azure**: Don't get hit, <BulletLink href={data.planners[7].url}>this clip</BulletLink> shows center dodge pattern.
- **Shadow Twin 2**:
  - Supports opposite blue soldier, DPS adjust to other safe spot.
  - <BulletLink href={data.planners[8].url}>ST 2 Raidplan</BulletLink>
