## Ketuduke
### Spring Crystals

> TR North / HM South

1. Bubbles start on their side in 2s; roots start in `2` for spread and `1` for stack.
2. Start on your side N/S (bubble gets pushed to the opposite side).
- To identify safe spots, find the crystal in the middle and make an H using it while looking at the long side. The 4 ends of the H are your safe squares.
- <BulletLink href={data.planners[0].url}>SC Image</BulletLink>

### Blowing Bubbles
- TR North / HM South; stay within your half of the room for the entire mech.
- **Stack > Spread**: Start middle, dodge to safe corner for stack; ragned stays for spread; melees run far.
- **Spread > Stack**: Start in the two safe squares on your half of the room, pre-position in the quadrant you want.
- <BulletLink href={data.planners[1].url}>BB movement Raidplan</BulletLink>
- <BulletLink href={data.planners[2].url}>BB in depth Raidplan</BulletLink>

### Adds
- Snake prio and color match add to a safe spot.
- TR N CW | HM W CCW, TM swapif duplicate bubble / root in pair.
- <BulletLink href={data.planners[3].url}>Adds Raidplan</BulletLink>

### Angry Seas
- HM W | TR E;
- Support north for spreads.
- Ranged take bubble across.
- Melee take middle tower if stack first.

  > You can use kb resist for melee uptime.
