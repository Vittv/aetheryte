## P1: Twintania

- <BulletLink href={data.planners[0].url}>Twintania Raidplan</BulletLink>

- **Prepull Setup**:

  ```markdown
  T1 | T2
  H1 | H2
  M1 | M2
  R1 | R2
  ```

- **Neurolink Placement**: Normal triangle: N, SW, SE (CCW, on 123 markers).
- Both tanks and 1 healer stay out of the first stack for LB cheese. Discuss which healer stays out.
-  <BulletLink href={data.planners[1].url}>Liquid Hells Raidplan</BulletLink> - Phys. Range always baits. Caster takes hatch if Phys. Range is first hatch.

## P2: Nael

- <BulletLink href={data.planners[2].url}>Nael Raidplan</BulletLink>
- **Party Movement**: Rotate **CW** around the boss by default.
- **Fire Order**: In > Out > In (2nd fire tether person out) > In.

- <BulletLink href={data.planners[3].url}>Divebombs Raidplan</BulletLink>

## P3: Bahamut

- <BulletLink href={data.planners[4].url}>Quickmarch Raidplan</BulletLink> - Uptime.
- <BulletLink href={data.planners[5].url}>Blackfire Raidplan</BulletLink> - DPS CCW, support CW.
- <BulletLink href={data.planners[6].url}>Fellruin Raidplan</BulletLink> - Party neuro opposite of Bahamut.
- <BulletLink href={data.planners[7].url}>Heavensfall Raidplan</BulletLink> - R1 tower under Nael.
- <BulletLink href={data.planners[8].url}>Tenstrike Raidplan</BulletLink> - Jump to indicate you're convering hatch, 1 always safe for Earthshakers.
- <BulletLink href={data.planners[9].url}>Grand Octet Raidplan</BulletLink> - Tank LB3, if no tank LB3 bait twin CCW (left) and stack CW of twin (right).

## P4: Adds

- <BulletLink href={data.planners[10].url}>Adds Raidplan</BulletLink>
- Discuss which boss you're taking (Default: MT Twin, OT Nael).
- Bosses between 1/3 markers, somewhat NE.
- All stacks done in the center of the arena.

## P5: Golden Bahamut

- <BulletLink href={data.planners[11].url}>Golden Bahamut Raidplan</BulletLink>
- **Akh Morn Order**: Share > MT invuln > OT invuln > Share.
- **Shared Busters**: Kitchen sinked with all available mitigation.
- **Mitigation Plan**:
  - 60s/90s CDs: Morn Afahs 1/3/5 or 2/4.
  - 2m CDs: Morn Afahs 1/4 or 2/5.
- Discuss with co-tank/melee/caster which Morn Afahs to mitigate.
- **Tank LBs**: No tank LBs in the default mitigation plan unless it's an emergency (e.g., < 8-person stack or invuln not up).
