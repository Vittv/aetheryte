## Party Finder Description

```markdown
DPS N SUPP S | box meteors | CW coronation | braindead flares
```

```markdown
DPS N SUPP S | L meteors | CW coronation | braindead flares
```

  - **L Meteors**: For the placement of Meteors during the "Earth" phase.
  - **CW Coronation**: During the Coronation Mechanic, the direction you take your tether from the triangle spawn location.
  - **Support South DPS North**: During the Hourglass platform with chains, Supports run South, DPS run North to break.
  - **Mit Authority**: Instead of dividing the party, heavy shields and mitigation are used for the stack and flare.
  - **Fixed Ice**: During the Ice phase with tethers. Players move in a fixed pattern according to which icicle they are tethered to.
  - **MTTT**: The order in which the tethers during "Ice" phase are taken.

### Raining Cats

1. Melee grabs tank tethers and go East and West.
2. Then they come back through the south towers, which will automatically pass the tethers to the tanks.
3. Tanks then just deal with the rest of the explosions East and West (mitigate).

## Phase 2

### Radical Shift

Since we're using the AFK markers, just stand on your marker, as center as possible, while not clipping your teammates and trying to stay inside as many glowy lines on the ground as possible. In theory, if everyone behaves and don't freak out, you should be able to not move at all until it resolves. **For the remainder of the phase, you can use your eyes and dodge and mit accordingly**.
