## Party Finder Description

```markdown
Hector | CC => CC
```

```markdown
Hector | CC => CW
```

- **Hector**: Refers to Hector (creator of the video guide), since we typically use his strategies in party finder.
- **CC → CC** or **CC → CW**: Directions that tethered players go during **Naught Hunts**. First tethered player always clockwise, second tethered players CW or CCW according to the chosen strategy.
