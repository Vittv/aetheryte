## Phase One

- **Chainblade Blow**: half-room cleave on the raised arm side, then ground AoE; go to other side
- **Wyvern's Siegeflight**: line AoE charge across arena
  - Red cracks: middle safe after dash
  - White cracks: sides safe after dash (also has rolling line AoE)
- **Rush**: three line AoE charges; charged spots explode in expanding rings; walk into one and dodge rings
- **Chainblade Charge**: stack on a random healer; share it
- **Towers** - six towers to soak; two large ones need tanks with mitigation
  - Bait the three sets of targeted AoEs first, then soak towers
  - Leftover spikes explode if hit by another mechanic
- **Wyvern's Vengeance**: four AoEs move outward from boss; dodge them
- **Wild Energy**: four players get spread AoEs; magic damage + short vuln debuff
- **Forged Fury**: enrage-style hit; applies **Guardian Will** (DoT + increased damage taken for rest of fight)

## Phase Two

- **Chainblade Blow**: now hits three times instead of one
- **Wild Energy**: now targets all eight players (except during towers)
- **Wyvern's Weal**: boss jumps to edge and fires a rotating laser; **Wild Energy** on all players simultaneously
  - Get to the side not hit by the laser, then spread for **Wild Energy** as it rotates
