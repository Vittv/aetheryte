
### Legend
  - **Hector**: We are using strats from the Hector guide.
  - **TF**: Toxic Friends raidplan.
  - **Kindred No Buddies**: Kindred Flatliner Strategy.
  - **Toxic No Buddies**: Toxic Friends Flatliner Strategy.
  - **No Buddies**: "Buddies" was an early strategy where the prey players pair up with tethers and mirror their tower knockbacks for Flatliner, **No buddies is not doing that**. 
  - **PTT or TTT or PPP**: Order of tower soaks at the end of the fight, P is party, T is tank. I.e. PTT = Party, Tank, Tank.
  - **Uptime or Fixed ES**: Two strategies for Ecliptic Stampede - the two fold fire or four fold fire mechanic.
